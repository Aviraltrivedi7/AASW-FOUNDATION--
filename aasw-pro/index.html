Who We Are

Our mission is to accelerate inclusive development across India.

AASW Foundation catalyses exchange of knowledge and best practices, facilitates meaningful partnerships, and promotes grassroots leadership to build a more equitable India.

  • Nurturing Education & Child Development
  • Women's Empowerment & Welfare
  • Catalysing Rural Health & Well-being
  • Building Sustainable Livelihoods
What We Do

Our Programs & Initiatives

Six integrated pillars creating lasting transformation in India's most underserved communities.

Shiksha Setu

Education

Bridging learning gaps for children in remote areas through community schools, digital classrooms, and scholarship support.

Arogya Gram

Health

Bringing preventive healthcare, mobile medical camps, and maternal wellness directly to underserved villages.

Shakti

Women

Empowering women with vocational skills, self-help groups, legal awareness, and entrepreneurship support.

Udyam

Livelihoods

Supporting farmers and rural youth with skill development, agri-tech, and market linkages to build sustainable incomes.

Kaushal

Skills

Market-aligned vocational training for rural youth — from digital literacy to trade skills — creating self-sufficient livelihoods.

Vittiya

Finance

Linking unbanked rural households to formal financial services — savings, credit, insurance, and government schemes.

Our Impact

Driven by People, Guided by Purpose

AASW Foundation is a unique nonprofit society owned, managed, and dedicated entirely to the benefit of India's most underserved communities — united by a common goal to innovate, catalyse meaningful partnerships, share knowledge, and achieve collective impact.

We are a bridge — connecting vulnerable communities with the resources, opportunities, and support systems they need to chart their own paths to dignity and lasting prosperity.

AASW Foundation Overview

Building an Equitable India, One Community at a Time

AASW Foundation unites communities, changemakers, and institutions around a cohesive vision of inclusive development. Our focused, evidence-based approach allows for meaningful progress across four Indian states.

We promote an inclusive approach — increasing diversity, accessibility, and sustainability in everything we do. We bring together professionals from government, civil society, and corporate India.
