Introduction

Bridging the digital divide for women in Uttar Pradesh

The chance for growth, leadership, and groundbreaking ideas belongs to everyone in today's fast-moving world—especially women from disadvantaged communities. Yet, deep-seated barriers still stop countless Indian women from finding their footing in technology, business, and sustainable careers.

AASW Foundation was founded to change this reality. We're a human-centered organization in the heart of Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to bridging the divide in digital business ownership.

Our Objective

To encourage women in digital entrepreneurship through educational avenues by providing digital tools, environmental awareness, and business mentoring.

Our Aim

Imagine women becoming inventors and decision-makers, using technology and business to create a sustainable, just society.

"Stronger women create a better world—socially, environmentally, and financially."
