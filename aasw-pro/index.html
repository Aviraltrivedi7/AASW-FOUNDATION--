About AASW Foundation

Fueling Women's Success Through Tech & Enterprise

The chance for growth, leadership, and groundbreaking ideas belongs to everyone in today's fast-moving world—especially women from disadvantaged communities. Yet, deep-seated barriers still stop countless Indian women from finding their footing in technology, business, and sustainable careers.

Aapka Apna Social Welfare Foundation was founded to change this reality. We're a human-centered organization in the heart of Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to bridging the divide in digital business ownership.

We're building a new wave of confident, capable, and connected women leaders by weaving together hands-on learning, practical skill-building, sincere mentorship, and a commitment to protecting our environment. Our core belief is simple: stronger women create a better world—socially, environmentally, and financially.

foundation The Foundation

Aapka Apna Social Welfare Foundation is a community-driven effort that directly helps women thrive in the digital economy. Based in Uttar Pradesh, our purpose is clear: to tackle the hurdles women face when seeking education, career opportunities, and leadership roles, especially in business and technology.

We don't just aim for inclusion; we work hard to create a space where women naturally step up as leaders. Through focused training, strong support systems, and local programs, we're giving women the tools they need to build lasting careers and make a real difference in their own neighborhoods.

target Our Objective

To encourage women in digital entrepreneurship through educational avenues by providing digital tools, environmental awareness, and business mentoring for promoting inclusive economic growth.

visibility Our Aim

Digital entrepreneurs exposing women throughout India now imagine them becoming inventors and decision-makers in the near future, using technology and business to create a sustainable, just society and empowered people.

flag Goals

  • ➢ Encourage the Indian states to actively support the AASW Foundation's programs and services.
  • ➢ By increasing our presence in both rural and urban areas, we can guarantee broad reach and involvement.
  • ➢ Give special attention to state-specific initiatives where women members might use creative social solutions.
  • ➢ Encourage awareness, self-sufficiency, and confidence in digital technology.
  • ➢ Document and disseminate success stories as inspiration for greater participation by more women in leadership and learning platforms.

work What We Do

Digital Skill Development

Women are trained in entrepreneurship: Digital skills, E-commerce, Social Media Marketing, and Online Operations enable women to start and maintain businesses.

Environmental Awareness

We provide environmental education with heart, focusing on ecologically conscious solutions and sustainable practices in business.

Mentorship & Support

Through our programs, mentors connect aspiring women entrepreneurs with professionals who can help guide them step-by-step in building and scaling their business.

Workshops & Seminars

This includes regular online and offline gatherings for leadership development, financial literacy, technology applications, branding, and digital innovation.

Building the Community

This growing network enables peer learning, collaboration, and confidence, especially for first-time entrepreneurs from remote locations.

account_balance Governance & Central Advisory Council

The Central Advisory Council acts as an important asset for the Aapka Apna Social Welfare Foundation to shape its strategy, integrity, and impact. The President of the Foundation is the Chair of the Central Advisory Council, and the Secretary acts as the Member Secretary. Thanks are extended from the bottom of our hearts to our members of the Advisory Council for their generosity, leadership, and commitment to the enhancement of our mission.

Key Functions:
  • ➢ Advises the Governing Board on key programs, outreach, and innovation;
  • ➢ Promotion of AASW Foundation initiatives across different states for ensuring visibility in a real sense;
  • ➢ Identifying unique local projects in different areas for members to implement tailor-made, community-driven solutions;
  • ➢ Generate awareness on various women-related issues and digital inclusion;
  • ➢ Facilitate seminars and events through organizational, logistical, and promotional assistance;
  • ➢ To assist in the publication of newsletters, achievements, and case studies to promote the progress of our members.
