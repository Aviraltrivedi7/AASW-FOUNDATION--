Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data

Data Protection

Your Privacy Matters

AASW Foundation respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data when you interact with our website, donate, or volunteer with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal identification information including your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you make a donation or subscribe to our newsletter. Payment details are processed securely by our trusted gateway partners (Razorpay/PayU) and are not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process donations and issue 80G tax limit receipts.
  • To send periodic updates, newsletters, and impact reports.
  • To improve our website functionality and user experience.

3. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties without your explicit consent.

4. Contacting Us

If there are any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us via our email at aaswfoundation06@gmail.com or by calling us at +91 9984156418.
